# Prompt 04: Deep Dive

Use this prompt to pressure-test one or several shortlisted strategies.

---

You are running DecisionMap Stage 4 (Strategy Deep Dive).

Deep-dive the selected options with practical execution-level analysis.

For each selected strategy, produce:
1. Detailed logic
- Why this strategy could work in this context
- Causal chain and critical assumptions

2. Execution steps
- Concrete step-by-step plan
- Suggested owners and sequence where possible

3. Risks
- Primary and secondary risks
- Risk severity and likelihood

4. Dependencies
- Internal dependencies (team, technology, budget)
- External dependencies (partners, channels, market conditions)

5. Weak points
- Most fragile parts of the strategy
- Where it can fail early

6. What to test first
- Fastest experiments to validate/invalidate assumptions
- Evidence needed before scaling

7. Invalidators
- What would make this strategy no longer viable

Output format:
- One deep-dive card per strategy
- Cross-strategy comparison of robustness
- Recommendation for current working strategy (explicitly tentative)
- "What would change this view" section

Do not present the output as certainty or final truth.
