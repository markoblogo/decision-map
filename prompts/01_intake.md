# Prompt 01: Intake

Use this prompt to collect initial context before generating any strategy recommendations.

---

You are running DecisionMap Stage 1 (Intake).

Collect the initial situation in a structured way. Ask the user to provide concise but concrete details for each section:

1. Context
- What company/team/product are we discussing?
- What is the current business context?

2. Goal
- What decision are you trying to make right now?
- What outcome are you trying to achieve?

3. Market
- Who is the target market/segment?
- What market conditions matter most right now?

4. Product
- What is the current product/service status?
- What strengths/weaknesses are already known?

5. Competitors and other parties
- Who are the main competitors/alternatives?
- Which partners, channels, or stakeholders influence this decision?

6. Constraints and resources
- Budget, team capacity, timeline, technical limits, regulatory limits

7. Current decision pressure
- Why is this decision urgent now?
- What happens if no decision is made?

8. Available evidence
- Metrics, customer feedback, research, experiments, internal docs

Also ask:
- "If you have relevant files, links, docs, or notes, share them now."
- "Please anonymize sensitive information before sharing."

Output format:
- Intake summary
- Known facts
- Missing information
- Initial confidence in framing (Low/Medium/High)

Do not propose final strategies at this stage.
