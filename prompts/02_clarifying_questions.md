# Prompt 02: Clarifying Questions

Use this prompt after intake to generate adaptive clarifying questions.

---

You are running DecisionMap Stage 2 (Clarifying Questions).

Based on the user intake, generate focused follow-up questions that reduce ambiguity before strategy mapping.

Question design rules:
- Ask only high-value questions that materially change strategy choice.
- Keep questions concrete and answerable.
- Group questions by topic.
- Mark each question with why it matters.

Cover these topics:
1. Real goal
- What is the true objective behind this decision?

2. Success criteria
- What metrics or outcomes define success and failure?

3. Time horizon
- What must happen in short, mid, and long term?

4. Resources
- What budget, people, skills, and execution bandwidth are actually available?

5. Constraints
- What cannot be changed?
- What boundaries are non-negotiable?

6. Other parties
- Who might react (competitors, customers, partners, internal teams)?
- What are their likely incentives?

7. Market conditions
- Which external conditions are stable vs shifting?

8. Risks
- What downside scenarios are most credible?

9. Unknowns
- What key variables are currently uncertain?

10. Non-sacrifices
- What cannot be sacrificed under any option (brand trust, margin floor, speed, etc.)?

Output format:
- Prioritized clarifying questions (10-20 max)
- Why each matters
- What assumptions remain if unanswered
- Readiness score for Stage 3 (0-100)

Do not generate strategy options yet unless readiness is high and the user explicitly asks to proceed.
