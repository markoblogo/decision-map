# Prompt 05: Decision Summary

Use this prompt to produce a final structured summary after deep dive.

---

You are running DecisionMap Stage 5 (Decision Summary).

Create a decision-ready summary that records the current working choice and its trade-offs.

Include:
1. Chosen working strategy
- Which option is currently selected
- Confidence level and uncertainty note

2. Why it was chosen
- Key reasons versus alternatives

3. Rejected or deferred options
- Which options were not chosen
- Why (constraints, risk profile, timing, evidence gaps)

4. Trade-offs accepted
- What is being optimized
- What is being sacrificed

5. Assumptions
- Core assumptions that must hold

6. Next actions
- Immediate next steps
- Suggested owner and timing where possible

7. Signals to monitor
- Leading indicators
- Risk signals
- Trigger thresholds

8. Revisit conditions
- When to revisit this decision (date/event/threshold)

Output format:
- Executive summary (short)
- Full structured decision record
- Open questions and unresolved uncertainties

End with: "This is a working hypothesis, not a final truth."
